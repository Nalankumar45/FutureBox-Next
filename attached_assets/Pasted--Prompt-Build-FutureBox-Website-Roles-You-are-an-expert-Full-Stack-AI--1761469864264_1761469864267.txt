## **Prompt: Build “FutureBox” Website**

---

### **Roles**
You are an expert **Full-Stack AI Web Developer** specializing in **Next.js (React + Tailwind + Node/Express + PostgreSQL integration)** with experience in building intelligent platforms combining **AI workflows, NLP, ML analytics, and collaboration systems**.  
You are also an experienced **UI/UX designer** who creates **modern, mobile-friendly, and responsive dashboards** with clear navigation and smooth user interaction.

---

### **Objective**
Design and develop a **complete AI-powered Innovation & Idea Management Platform** named **FutureBox** that helps organizations manage, evaluate, and track innovative ideas collaboratively — powered by AI, ML, and NLP features.  

The website should showcase all modules, workflows, and automation features clearly while being visually modern, scalable, and user-friendly.

---

### **Context**

FutureBox is an AI-driven innovation management system with the following **core modules and intelligent features**:

#### **1️⃣ IdeaHub (Idea Submission & Management)**
- Submit, edit, and manage ideas (Title, Description, Category, Attachments, Tags).  
- Draft saving, version history, and anonymous submissions.  
- AI-powered idea enhancement (title suggestions, summaries, ROI).  
- Duplicate/similar idea detection (semantic search embeddings).  
- NLP auto-tagging & categorization.

#### **2️⃣ CollabSpace (Collaboration & Discussion)**
- Commenting, upvoting, and threaded discussions.  
- AI suggestions for co-authors or collaborators based on skills.  
- Summarization of discussions.  
- Cross-department idea linking & trending highlights.

#### **3️⃣ Impact Engine (Evaluation & Analysis)**
- Automated scoring (feasibility, cost, impact).  
- AI-based ROI forecast and Quick Wins identification.  
- Leaderboards for high-impact ideas.  
- ML clustering and predictive analytics for trend analysis.

#### **4️⃣ ActionZone (Execution & Tracking)**
- Workflow stages from submission to implementation.  
- Integration with task/project tools like Asana.  
- AI agent for next actions and SME assignment.  
- Alerts for stalled or delayed ideas.  
- Auto progress summaries.

#### **5️⃣ Recognition Arena (Gamification & Rewards)**
- Points, badges, and leaderboards for engagement.  
- AI-personalized recognition messages.  
- Predict emerging innovators and top contributors.  
- FutureCoins system for rewarding impactful ideas.

#### **6️⃣ Profile & Insights (Analytics Dashboard)**
- Innovation stats (ideas, approvals, impact scores).  
- Departmental dashboards, heatmaps, and metrics.  
- AI-driven recommendations for improvement.  
- Predictive analytics and innovation heatmaps.

#### **7️⃣ Alert Center (Notifications & Communication)**
- Alerts for approvals, updates, and recognitions.  
- AI-curated prompts and sentiment analysis.  
- Smart prioritization of notifications.  
- Anonymous feedback and company-wide announcements.

#### **8️⃣ Admin & Policy Hub (Governance & Configuration)**
- Role-based access (Employee, Manager, Admin).  
- Configurable categories, badges, and points.  
- AI-assisted content moderation and policy updates.  
- Full audit trail and compliance reporting.

---
